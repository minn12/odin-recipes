How to Make Easy Burrito Bowl!

Discription

Ever wanted to make a quick an easy burrito? Well look no further this is an easy burrito bowl recipe that saves tons of time. NO more going to Guzman Y Gomez, NO more seceret abuela. receipes.

A dish satisfies everyone in the family (maybe not your abuela) Try it tonight!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon fajita seasoning
  • 1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (8 ounce) jar salsa
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • Â½ cup shredded Mexican cheese blend

Steps

  1. Heat oil n a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 10 minutes; drain and discard grease.
  2. Stir fajita seasoning into ground beef until evenly coated.
  3. Mix pinto beans, salsa, and brown rice into ground beef mixture.
  4. Top with Mexican cheese blend.
