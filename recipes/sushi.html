How to Make Easy Sushi Roll!

Discription

Ever wanted to make a quick and easy sushi roll? Well look no further this is an easy sushi recipe that saves tons of time. NO more going into sushi stores for authentic japanese stuff, NO more waiting for a sushi on the sushi train, just for it to be taken by someone else.

A dish satisfies only you! Try it tonight!

Ingredients

  • 1 ⅓ cups water
  • ⅔ cup uncooked short-grain white rice
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 4 sheets nori seaweed sheets
  • ½ pound imitation crabmeat, flaked
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • ½ cucumber, peeled, cut into small strips
  • 2 tablespoons pickled ginger
